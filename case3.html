Product Overview

The Football Ground Reservation Website simplifies the process of booking football grounds by offering real-time availability, detailed pricing, and a user-friendly interface. It enables users to quickly secure their preferred slots while providing options for advance payments, reducing confusion and enhancing overall satisfaction. The app improves user experience and ground management by offering up-to-date information and seamless payment options.

Category

Website

Project Duration

12th October - 30th October

My Role

UX Designer

My Responsibilty

Interviewing, digital and paper wireframing, low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototyping, usability testing, and iterating on designs.

Target Users

Football Enthusiasts

Persona

Problem Statement

Tashi is a football enthusiast who enjoys playing on weekends with friends but struggles with existing reservation apps. These apps often fail to display accurate ground availability and are difficult to navigate, leading to frustration and missed opportunities. Tashi needs a user-friendly app that allows him to quickly view available slots and secure bookings without hassle.

Problem Statement

Sonam frequently plans last-minute games with friends but often feels frustrated by complex and slow-loading reservation apps. She values a fast, straightforward app that clearly displays availability and allows her to make quick bookings with minimal effort.

User Journey Map

Wireframe

Paper Wireframe

The paper wireframe provides a foundational visual layout for the football ground reservation app, illustrating how users can easily access availability, select their preferred slots, and complete the booking process. This simple and intuitive design helps identify key interactions and user needs, ensuring that the app effectively supports a seamless booking experience.

Low-Fidelity Prototype

The low-fidelity prototype presents a basic interactive framework for the app. This user flow addresses the needs of users by enabling them to view available slots, select and reserve grounds, and complete their bookings. The prototype emphasizes a straightforward design that simplifies the reservation process, ensuring users can navigate with ease while retaining essential functionality.

High-Fidelity Wireframe

The high-fidelity prototype serves as an interactive representation of the football ground reservation app. This user flow allows users to easily view available time slots, select and reserve grounds, and complete their bookings securely, enhancing the overall experience while streamlining the reservation process.

Usuability Study

I conducted two rounds of usability testing, one on the low-fidelity prototype and another on the high-fidelity version of the app. These tests were crucial for understanding user interactions and pain points. Insights from these studies guided me in refining the design to better meet user needs.

Round 1 Findings

Too many steps to book a ground; users felt overwhelmed by the number of steps involved.

Round 2 Findings

Inconsistent color scheme

Takeaways

Impact

Designing the football ground reservation app improved both user experience and ground management. It made the booking process more convenient, reducing wait times and manual effort. Users enjoyed selecting preferred slots and received real-time updates, leading to higher satisfaction and loyalty.

What I Learned

I gained experience in balancing simplicity and functionality, prioritizing accessibility, refining designs through user feedback, collaborating effectively with developers, and focusing on mobile-first design for a responsive user experience.
